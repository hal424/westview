About Us

Westview Industries, Inc.

We are a professionally aggressive company with a solid track record of consistently achieving and exceeding our manufacturers’ goals throughout our account base. We firmly believe that our ongoing successful involvement and knowledge of the buying community will be a benefit to your company.

As a sales organization we have developed a unique understanding of this strategic and volatile marketplace. Our region is the headquarters for many important national accounts, corporate buying offices, chain stores, department stores, mass merchants, distributors, direct mail, E-commerce merchants, computer and specialty retailers.

Each customer is unique as well. They require us to thoroughly understand their management’s policies and work within their guidelines. We have therefore established close relationships with the merchandising team and executive management level in each account in order to maximize our results and accomplish the goals set forth by our manufacturers.

There is a delicate balance that must be achieved and maintained between our customers and manufacturers. This is specifically a function, we, as manufacturers representitives, perform in a highly productive fashion.





Work with Us!

A plethora of reasons.

We are dedicated to you and your success. Our commitment and values are outlined below. ￼￼￼￼￼￼￼￼

Attention

We have the desire, time and ability to properly give the attention necessary to achieve our mutual goals. We represent a select group of key manufaturers and can assure full commitment deserved to maintain focus and be effective.

Distribution

We are actively servicing and selling every class of trade. We can increase sales and distribution through new accounts and expand business within the current account base. Targeted accounts deemed meaningful to expand your business will be our focus under your direction.

Commitment

Participation by everyone including principals. Total involvement on a daily basis by the entire staff. Thorough and comprehensive knowledge and familiarity of account base and vendors by all, including inside support staff.

Vendor Trust

Manufacturers enjoy working with us because of our professionalism, follow up, commitment, tenacity, close relationships with customers and our long history of positive end results; a healthy and growing business.

Follow Up

We write and follow through with correspondence which clarifies the orders, projections, product flows, ad dates and commitments by all parties. We confirm purchase orders and follow up to insure timely delivery. We develop and implement logical systems, manually and electronically, with each vendor, which facilitates smooth processing of orders. We are in constant communication with all levels of personnel at all times.

Detailing & Service

In-store visits to assure proper placement and display and to provide accurate and timely feedback in the form of useful information needed for buyers and sales personnel. Dissemination of product knowledge through seminars and training sessions. Regular mailings to account base and projections for manufacturers on a regular rolling schedule based on sell-through information and market trends.
